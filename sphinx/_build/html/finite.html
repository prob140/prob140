Finite Distribution (prob140.FiniteDistribution)¶

FiniteDistribution Subclass of DiscreteDistribution to represent a Finite Probability Distribution.
class prob140.FiniteDistribution(labels=None, _deprecated=None, *, formatter=<datascience.formats.Formatter object>)[source]¶

Subclass of DiscreteDistribution to represent a Finite Probability Distribution.

Construct a FiniteDistribution by specifying both the range of the domain and the associated probabilities

Examples

>>> FiniteDistribution().domain(make_array(2, 3, 4)).probability(make_array(0.25, 0.5, 0.25))
Domain | Probability
2      | 0.25
3      | 0.5
4      | 0.25

The domain method takes in an iterable. To associate each value with a probability, the probability method takes an iterable of the same length

>>> FiniteDistribution().domain(np.arange(1,11)).probability_function(lambda x:1/10)
Domain | Probability
1      | 0.1
2      | 0.1
3      | 0.1
4      | 0.1
5      | 0.1
6      | 0.1
7      | 0.1
8      | 0.1
9      | 0.1
10     | 0.1

FiniteDistribution can also use a function on the domain to assign probabilities

Constucting¶

FiniteDistribution.domain(values) Assigns domain values to a FiniteDistribution
FiniteDistribution.probability(values) Assigns probabilities to domain values.
FiniteDistribution.probability_function(pfunc) Assigns probabilities to a FiniteDistribution via a probability function.

Utitilies¶

DiscreteDistribution.prob_event(x) Finds the probability of an event x
DiscreteDistribution.event(x) Shows the probability that distribution takes on value x or range of values x.
FiniteDistribution.expected_value() Finds expected value of distribution
FiniteDistribution.sd() Finds standard deviation of FiniteDistribution
FiniteDistribution.variance() Finds variance of distribution
FiniteDistribution.normalize() Normalizes the distribution by making the proabilities sum to 1
